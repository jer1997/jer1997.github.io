ABOUT THE PROJECT

For my college-level Writing and Digital Media course, I worked with my classmates Meredith Huddle and Phoebe Myers to explore mental health resources on our college campus. In the process, we learned that students themselves are vital to keeping our campus happy and healthy, and that the more conversations we have about mental health, the better. I hope this portfolio sparks some conversations of your own!
